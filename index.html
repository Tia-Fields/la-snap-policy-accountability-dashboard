Matched Administrative and Legislative Records: Methodology

District-level SNAP Participation: Participation figures are derived from administrative records matched to 105 House and 39 Senate seats. Data is normalized across PAR rosters and participation reports for 100% seat coverage.

Fiscal Exposure Modeling: The $95M+ figure is calculated based on the delta between the estimated PER and the 6.0% federal threshold, applied to total annual SNAP outlays subject to H.R.1 penalty triggers.

Payment Error Rates (PER): Data points represent state and federal Quality Control (QC) findings. 2025-2026 figures are projections based on historical operational friction and administrative churn levels.

Administrative Burden: Closure reasons are categorized by state procedural codes to differentiate between eligibility-based exits and administrative churn.
